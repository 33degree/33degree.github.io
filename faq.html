FAQ

What is so special about this conference?

Developers are very lucky as their average salary is very high. At the same time most of the developers would like to improve their skills and give something back to those who need help. We would like to match the need of constant and strong need for improvements and learning with the possibility to support charities.

How much is the ticket for 33rd Degree 4 Charity?

You decide how much you would like to give. It might be 61.50, 123, 184.50, 246, 307.50, 369 zł or 15, 30, 45, 60, 75, 90 euro.

What's the schedule?

You can see it on our main page.

What charity organizations are you supporting?

Attendees of conference will support 4 foundations: Fundacja Mam Marzenie, Rak'n'Roll, Szlachetna Paczka and Devoxx4kids. You can read some more about them on our main page.
